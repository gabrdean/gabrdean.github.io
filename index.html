About me

I once was the gamer who'd stay up from sun up till sundown neglecting my bodily needs. It was an addiciton that I proudly overcame. Though the path left me with an affinity for technology. I was disgusted in my satisfaction with in-game achievenments while my life (external game) suffered so much. I had to look for that lure in life somehow. I eventually found a liking in teaching and helping others better facilitate their lives with technology. I went from jailbreaking ipods and emaulating multiplayer enabled psp games on my old dell laptop, to eventually teaching students how to use microcontrollers (Arduino). To this day, I provide freedom to the people around me with my skillset. Creating external value as opposed to in-game achievements. I'm now a graduate of App Academy and Fullstack Developer.
